Welcome

Schöneberg is one of the most beloved neighborhoods in Berlin with a rich and proud history. From its origins as a small countryside village in the 13th century to its inclusion into greater Berlin, the inhabitants of Schöneberg have always represented religious, cultural and economic diversities. Diversity is the leitmotif of this neighborhood and we, as proud Schöeneberger, have created these three different tours to let you experience the multifaceted history of this fascinating neighborhood. Take a look down this page to see our offer of free walking tours!
