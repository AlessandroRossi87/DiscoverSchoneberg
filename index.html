Welcome


Schöneberg, a beloved neighborhood in Berlin, boasts a captivating history and rich cultural heritage. Initially a quaint countryside village in the 13th century, it eventually became an integral part of greater Berlin, embodying religious, cultural, and economic diversity throughout its journey.


At the heart of Schöneberg lies its remarkable diversity, which serves as the guiding principle for our community. As proud Schönebergers, we have curated three distinct walking tours, each offering a unique perspective on the multifaceted history of this fascinating neighborhood. We invite you to explore our range of free walking tours listed below.


All tours start from John-F.-Kennedy-Platz
