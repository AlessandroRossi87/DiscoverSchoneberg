Welcome


Schöneberg is one of the most beloved neighborhoods in Berlin with a rich and proud history. From its origins as a small countryside village in the 13th century to its inclusion into greater Berlin, the inhabitants of Schöneberg have always represented religious, cultural and economic diversities.


Diversity is the leitmotif of this neighborhood and we, as proud Schöeneberger, have created these three different tours to let you experience the multifaceted history of this fascinating neighborhood. Take a look down this page to see our offer of free walking tours!

Tour 1
Women of Resistance


Discover the history of the many women who lived in Schöneberg and contributed to the resistance movement between 1933 and 1945!

Tour 2
Schöneberg and its buildings


A walk through the most iconic landmarks of Schöneberg, and the people connected to them!

Tour 3
Queer History


Schöneberg has a 130 year history of queer life and resistance, join this tour to discover the oldest rainbow quarter of the world!
